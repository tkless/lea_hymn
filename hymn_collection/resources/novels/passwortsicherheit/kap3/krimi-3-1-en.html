Sensitive Data


"And—what is it?" Patrick asked impatiently while his grandmother stared transfixed at columns of numbers scrolling across the screen as she moved the mouse up and down or jumped from one window to another.

"Well," she said hesitantly, adjusting her glasses. "I'm not really an expert, but this seems to be about financial transactions of various companies that shouldn't actually be on the same data carrier. Except in the case of an external financial service provider—or industrial espionage."

Patrick's eyes widened. Were they on the trail of a large-scale financial crime? A cold shiver ran down his spine.
