Introduction

The present Hypermedia Novel (HYMN) for Topic Password Security offers the opportunity to go through the story non-linear paths across different storylines - here genre track.
The plots or narrative strands can be used via the icon under the tab narrative strand can be changed at will.
In total, there are six options in this novel to choose narrative lines.
Here is a brief outline of each Storylines:

Standard - In this narrative thread the The story is structured generically and is not specific.

Crime - Here the narrative about password security is presented as a small one Crime story presented.

Science fiction - A fiction about settlement and protecting a habitat on the planet Mars.

Mystery - A secret order of monks who have been... A secret about an enchanted ring has been kept for generations.

Fantasy - While teaching a sorcerer's apprentice unexpectedly something goes wrong...

Comedy - A surprise party threatens to collapse, as the password for the digitally secured planning documents forgotten.

You can find the narrative you have chosen under the respective content (also under this one) in the footer.

In addition to the narrative threads described above, there is one at the end of the storyline Background information chapter.

This section provides scientifically collected information about the creation and importance of secure passwords, as well as techniques used “Cracking” the passwords.
The outlook deals with password managers and the influence of the Quantum computing with regard to “cracking” passwords.
